About

scmpuff is a minimalistic reimplementation of the core functionality from SCM Breeze.

Its focus is on simplicity, speed, robustness, and cross-platform support.

It's free and open source, like most good things in life.

Learn more on GitHub »

Installation

Download for your platform, and copy the binary to /usr/local/bin or somewhere else in your default $PATH.

Then run scmpuff init for instructions to activate in your shell.
